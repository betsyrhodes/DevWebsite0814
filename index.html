Sound Designer turned Web Developer

about me...

As a sound designer I have had the opportunity to collaborate with some of the most cutting edge theater companies in New York city and have designed world premieres such as Steve Martin’s adaptation of the Underpants and John Patrick Shanley’s Dirty Story, along with classics such as The Winter’s Tale starring Oscar-nominee David Strathairn. I am now looking to expand my career beyond the stage. During the past year I took a sabbatical from my design work to focus on becoming a programmer. I have just graduated from Dev Bootcamp and am now seeking oportunities to merge my love of music, storytelling, and programming.
